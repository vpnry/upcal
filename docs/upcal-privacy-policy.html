Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how upCal handles user and device data.

By using the app, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Location Privacy

This app itself does not collect, store, or track your location data (latitude and longitude).

However, when certain optional features are used — such as satellite imagery, street maps, or automatic location name detection — your device's GPS coordinates may be processed by third-party services. These services require an internet connection.

1.1 Satellite & Aerial Imagery

Satellite and aerial imagery are provided by ArcGIS Online:

server.arcgisonline.com/ArcGIS/rest/services/World_Imagery/MapServer

Use of these services is subject to Esri's terms and policies.

1.2 Street Map Tiles

Street map tiles are provided by OpenStreetMap public tile servers:

tile.openstreetmap.org

Use of these services is subject to OpenStreetMap's terms and policies.

1.3 Automatic Location Name Detection

City names, regions, and other geographic labels may be resolved using the Flutter Geocoding plugin:

pub.dev/packages/geocoding
